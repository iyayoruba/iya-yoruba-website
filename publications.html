Projects.

The institutions, platforms, and archives built to ensure Yorùbá language and culture survive — and thrive.

YT
Digital Archive & Library

YorubaTexts

Yorùbá publications are disappearing. Decades of writing — from the 1900s through the early 2000s — are out of print, scattered across private collections, church shelves, and institutional libraries with no single place to trace them. Most of what exists is not even known to exist.

YorubaTexts is building the most comprehensive digital catalog of Yorùbá-language literature ever assembled, spanning publications from 1938 to the present. It is the foundation of something larger: a digital library where every Yorùbá text ever published can be found and reached, regardless of where in the world you are.

  • Comprehensive catalog spanning 1938 to present
  • Mapping holdings across U.S. institutional libraries
  • Community-powered submissions and verification
  • Foundation for open-access Yorùbá digital library

AL
Online Language Institute

Alámọ̀já Languages

Founded in 2018 with no institutional backing — just a conviction that Yorùbá learners worldwide deserved structured, culturally grounded, expert-led instruction. Today Alámọ̀já Languages is a fully operational institute with a team of 15+, serving learners at every level across three continents.

The institute offers teaching in Yorùbá language and culture — from complete beginners to advanced learners — with a curriculum built around cultural immersion, not just grammar.

  • Structured curriculum from beginner to advanced
  • Live interactive instruction and recorded modules
  • Heritage and L2 learner programmes
  • Children's Yorùbá education track
  • Certification pathways

YV
Digital Learning Platform

Yoruversity

A self-paced digital learning platform for Yorùbá language, literature, and culture. Designed for learners who want flexibility without sacrificing depth — interactive lessons, multimedia resources, and a community of learners at every stage.

  • Self-paced learning modules
  • Video lessons and audio resources
  • Interactive exercises and assessments
  • Community forums and peer support

Collection of Yorùbá Verbs

One of very few published Yorùbá language learning resources commercially available in English-language markets. A practical reference for learners and educators — self-published and available on Amazon KDP since 2021.

  • Comprehensive verb reference for learners
  • Available on Amazon Kindle
  • Designed for both classroom and self-study use
