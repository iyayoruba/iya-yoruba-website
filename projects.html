Digital Archive & Library

YorubaTexts

Yorùbá publications are disappearing. Decades of writing are out of print, scattered across private collections, church shelves, and institutional libraries with no single place to trace them. Most of what exists is not even known to exist. YorubaTexts is building the most comprehensive digital catalog of Yorùbá-language literature ever assembled, spanning publications from 1938 to the present. It is the foundation of something larger: a digital library where every Yorùbá text ever published can be found and reached, regardless of where in the world you are.

  • Comprehensive catalog spanning 1938 to present
  • Mapping holdings across U.S. institutional libraries
  • Community-powered submissions and verification
  • Foundation for open-access Yorùbá digital library

Online Language Institute

Alámọ̀já Languages

Founded in 2018 with no institutional backing, Alámọ̀já Languages began as Alámọ̀já Yorùbá from a single conviction that Yorùbá learners worldwide deserved structured, culturally grounded, expert-led instruction. That conviction has grown. Today Alámọ̀já Languages is a fully operational institute teaching Yorùbá, Igbo, and expanding to other African languages with a team of 20+ serving learners across continents.

  • Structured curriculum from beginner to advanced
  • Live interactive instruction and recorded modules
  • Heritage and L2 learner programmes
  • Children's Yorùbá education track
  • Certification pathways

Digital Learning Platform

Yoruversity

A self-paced digital learning platform for Yorùbá language, literature, and culture. Designed for learners who want flexibility without sacrificing depth — interactive lessons, multimedia resources, and a community of learners at every stage.

  • Self-paced learning modules
  • Video lessons and audio resources
  • Interactive exercises and assessments
  • Community forums and peer support
